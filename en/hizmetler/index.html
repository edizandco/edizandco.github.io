FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the delivery timeline really 5 business days?
Yes. The timeline begins once 100% payment is completed and all base assets (logo, copy, imagery) are provided. Setups and launch are completed within 5 business days.
Who pays for domain name and hosting?
First-year domain, hosting (Hostinger infrastructure), and SSL certificate costs are included in the package price. Starting from the second year, a flat annual service fee of 3,000 TL is charged for hosting, server management, and technical support. You can also transfer the infrastructure to your own account after the first year if you wish.
Why do you write code from scratch instead of using website builders?
We write optimized, lightweight custom code that loads instantly, stays incredibly secure, and avoids platform lock-in. This keeps your operating costs minimal while providing maximum customizability.
Why is payment required 100% upfront?
To maintain our operational focus and keep our delivery speed at maximum, we do not work with partial payments or open account models. Our process officially begins after the payment is completed.
What happens if we delay our content?
You must provide your content within a maximum of 7 calendar days after payment. If this period is exceeded, the project is suspended due to operational capacity reservation, and no refund will be issued.
How do we make updates after delivery?
We connect the editable areas of your website (contact info, pricing, address, etc.) to a custom Google Sheets (Excel) file. When you update the text in this sheet, your website updates automatically in real-time. You do not need coding knowledge or to pay us extra fees. For design updates, you can always request support.
When will our website be listed on Google search?
We deliver your site with a complete technical SEO foundation and sitemaps. However, the indexing timeline is controlled entirely by search engine algorithms and typically ranges from a few days to a few weeks.
